Hi, We are Team IMMUNIS

A young girl named Alice , faced a challenge when she was struck by a rare and incurable virus. Just when hope seemed lost, Dr. Jekyll, a brilliant and compassionate scientist, formed a groundbreaking team named IMMUNIS. Their mission? To shrink themselves and venture inside Alice's body, embarking on an extraordinary journey to eradicate the virus and restore her health. This daring and innovative approach held the promise of a miraculous recovery, lighting a spark of hope in Alice's heart. Now, it's your turn, Guardian! Join the game now and help IMMUNIS in their mission to save Alice!
